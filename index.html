An Overview

"IF" by Davido is a romantic Afrobeat song released in 2017 that became a massive hit across Africa and beyond. The song expresses deep affection, as Davido pledges to go to great lengths, even spending "30 billion for your account," to prove his love. With its infectious rhythm, catchy lyrics, and smooth delivery, IF quickly became an anthem for lovers and partygoers alike.
